Welcome to the FriendFinder Survey!

To begin, please type in your name and provide a photo url for your profile image. After that, you will find a survey form comprised of 10 different survey questions. Below each question, you will see a scale of 1 to 5. Choose a number based on how much you agree or disagree with the question, with 1 being strongly disagree, and 5 being strongly agree.

Once finished, click the submit button, and FriendFinder will find you your perfect match! Click here if you'd like to go back to the homepage.
